1. dir - Helps us to see what are the folders inside the given directory.
2. mkdir - Helps us to create a folder.
3. rmdir - Helps us to remove directory.
4. pwd - Helps us know the current path.
5. cd - Helps us to change directory.
6. echo. >NewFile - Helps us to create a NewFile.
7. move - Helps us to move a folder to another folder.
8. xcopy - Helps us to copy a directory.
